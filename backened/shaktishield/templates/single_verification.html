About Single Certificate Verification

This portal is designed for verifying individual certificates issued by the Jharkhand Academic Council. It provides secure authentication of academic documents to ensure their validity and authenticity.

Verification Features

  • Security feature validation
  • Real-time verification status
  • Detailed verification reports

  • Secure database verification
  • Audit trail maintenance
